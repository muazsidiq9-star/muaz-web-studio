Project Overview

The Al-Bayan Landing Page was created as a clean entry point for prospective students to understand the institute’s programs and apply easily.

It focuses on clarity, trust-building, and guiding users toward enrollment.

The Challenge

  • No strong digital presence for student acquisition
  • Low engagement from previous web structure
  • No clear admission funnel
  • Poor mobile conversion experience

Our Approach

  • Conversion-focused landing page design
  • Clear hierarchy for programs and benefits
  • Strong call-to-action placement
  • Mobile-first responsive layout

Key Features

Institution Branding
