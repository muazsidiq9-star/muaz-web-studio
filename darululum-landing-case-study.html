Project Overview

The Dar Al-Ulum landing page was designed as a focused marketing entry point for the institute, highlighting programs and guiding users toward admission.

Unlike full systems, this page prioritizes clarity, persuasion, and fast user action.

The Challenge

  • No structured online presence for admissions
  • Lack of conversion-focused design
  • Poor mobile experience in previous pages
  • No clear call-to-action flow

Our Approach

  • Clean modern landing page design
  • Strong CTA placement strategy
  • Mobile-first responsive layout
  • Fast-loading optimized structure
